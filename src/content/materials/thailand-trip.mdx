## The story

People often say that traveling opens your eyes to new worlds and gives you fresh perspectives. I had always known this to be true, but it was not until my recent trip to Thailand that I truly understood the meaning behind those words.

This was not my first visit to Thailand, but it was by far the most memorable one. I experienced a traditional Thai massage, rode an elephant through lush surroundings, fed playful animals at the zoo, and enjoyed countless hours of shopping. However, the highlight of the entire trip was a single photograph — one in which an elephant wrapped its long trunk around me in what felt like a warm embrace. I had never imagined that I would have the chance to be hugged by an elephant! That unexpected moment is what made the whole journey unforgettable.

Before this trip, I had never seriously considered revisiting Thailand. Now, I find myself eager to return before the end of this year. I hope this wish will come true someday — because some destinations deserve more than one visit.
