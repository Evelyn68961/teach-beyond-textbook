## The story

### A trip of memories

Yesterday morning, I stumbled upon a dusty photo album while organizing several shelves in my room. I had almost no recollection of it. Out of curiosity, I gently opened the old album. All of a sudden, my childhood memories came flooding back — a gentle, smiling face gazed at me from the pages…… I found myself drawn into a journey of memories, carried along by a quiet sense of peace and warmth.

### A peaceful encounter in kindergarten

Jola — a girl with long brown hair — is my dearest friend. I first met her on my very first day of kindergarten. Before I had a chance to adapt to this unfamiliar environment, my mind was filled with fear and anxiety. While everyone in class was choosing activities to attend, I noticed a line of small print by accident: "Why not learn Go with me? Let's discover the secret behind the chess! — Ms. Lee"

Play Go? I signed up for the club, filled with a mixture of uncertainty and curiosity……

On my first day at the Go club, I was assigned to the same group as a girl with bright, pretty eyes. After a simple greeting, we began learning Go under Ms. Lee and played happily together. Before we knew it, our first lesson had come to an end. The girl, named Jola, turned out to be fantastic at painting and also passionate about reading. Because of our shared interests, we quickly became close friends. And to this day, our friendship has never wavered.

What surprised me even more was how fascinating the Go club turned out to be. Although it was a little challenging for me at first, I made rapid progress under the teacher's patient, step-by-step guidance.

### Reflection after a game

Go is truly a remarkable game that not only challenges our thinking ability but also provides valuable opportunities to learn how to approach difficulties calmly and independently.

Go is a wonderful hobby that has strengthened both my friendships and my abilities — I closed the album softly, still immersed in these cherished memories.
