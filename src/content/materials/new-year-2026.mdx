## The story

### New Year's Eve

"Happy New Year!" A cheerful message from Jola caught my attention. "I just begged my parents to swing by the grocery store on our way to our hometown!" Her voice gradually rose as she shared the news that filled her with enthusiasm and joy. "Do you believe it? They agreed without hesitation!" She continued excitedly, "They said I should get a taste of setting off firecrackers, so I'm picking them out with my family right now. How lucky I am!"

Jola had told me before that she longed to set off firecrackers after watching the video of my experience. After congratulating her, I turned off the iPad. Her first wish for the new year had come true, and she wanted to fully immerse herself in the moment. I know I'm the only one who knows this little secret of hers. But what about me? I started thinking about the question with a mix of anxiety and curiosity, then went back to enjoying myself with a magazine and a cup of hot chocolate…

### My New Achievement and Hope in 2026

"Your new goals for 2026—" A phrase in the magazine pulled me into a moment of deep thought, and I found myself struggling with my uncertain achievements. A new year, a new start — a new goal to achieve is indeed needed. Embarking on a new journey, countless hurdles and difficulties may appear, but I hope I can overcome them with a smile and let the challenges sharpen my skills. The dawn crept over me quietly. How peaceful it was! I kept writing my new hopes and goals for 2026 with a confident smile while basking in the warm sunlight. I won't limit myself anymore. Now, why don't you finish your goal list with me? "2026, here we go!"
