## The story

"Lately, I've been so troubled that I can't fall asleep at night." Ema complained about the pressures of her career, her cheeks flushed with frustration. "It's such a huge hassle, and it's really overwhelming me…… I'd love to know your opinion on this matter." She sprawled on the sofa lazily, her eyes brimming with helplessness and sadness.

I was well aware of the contradiction in her words. Deep down, she didn't really want to discuss this problem with me. Therefore, I decided to keep silent rather than chatter away; letting her pour out her feelings would be far more effective in soothing her emotions.

Ema is one of my relatives, a twenty-year-old office worker. Just last week, she landed a highly paid position, yet she has been wavering ever since — should she cling to the stability of her steady job, or venture into a more challenging realm and risk everything she has built?

This semester at school, each sixth-grade class has to collaborate on a ten-minute drama to showcase six years of learning. Enthusiasm, courage, responsibility, and cooperation — all are put to the test. From composing a draft and rehearsing, to recording the final performance, the teachers will vote for the most marvelous piece in each category, and the first-place winner will be rewarded with a prize. Everyone is ready to conquer this final task, including me…… Including me?

Because of my resonant voice, I was assigned to Group A, the performance group, and was made responsible for acting on stage. Although I usually prefer to draft scripts and edit videos behind the scenes, I agreed anyway — my agreement sprang from a genuine desire to try something new.

However, things didn't unfold as I had expected. Today marks the "fifth" day of the competition. Although my performances are not too terrible, I grow more exhausted and overwhelmed with each passing day. No one suspects that I'm not actually good at acting; I even feel a flutter of nerves whenever I step onto the stage.

Finally, I explained my struggles carefully to the leader of my class, and was granted permission to join the editing group. I'm beginning to realize my previous decision was foolish. The journey itself will always be arduous; the only things within our power to change are the path we embark on, the courage we summon to overcome each hurdle, the approach we take, and the way we see our own life……
