## The story

Two months ago, a young lady in a white blouse rushed into the teacher's office and bumped right into me as I was heading back to my classroom after handing in my homework. "Ah! I'm really sorry," she apologized, glancing at my reports now scattered all over the floor. "No, no… it was my own carelessness for carrying too many papers while walking," I replied. As I knelt down to gather them, I suddenly noticed something strange — she didn't look like any of the teachers at our school. Then she smiled brightly and introduced herself: "I'm Lily, your new English teacher. Lovely to meet you!"

### The Qualities of a Marvelous Teacher — Enthusiasm, Patience, and Responsibility

"Hey class, please take out your textbook!" From that day on, every time the English class bell rang, Miss Lily would dash into the classroom and dive straight into the lesson. Bit by bit, we all grew accustomed to this somewhat impetuous yet endearing new teacher. Although she could be a little clumsy and shy, she always brought a refreshing touch of innocence to our English class, quietly igniting our passion for the language without ever raising her voice. To me, she is by far the most warm-hearted and responsible teacher I have ever met.

### Self-Control vs. Self-Limitation

One afternoon, I was wrestling with the question of whether or not to join the summer recreation camp. I knew it would be a wonderful chance to build my independence, yet a part of me wondered if that same stretch of time might be better spent on something more meaningful. As I sat at my desk staring blankly into space, a gentle voice broke through my thoughts: "Follow your heart!" Miss Lily had quietly walked over, her eyes calm but resolute. "You feel hesitant because you're afraid of wasting time." She paused, and her gaze softened. "But what if it doesn't waste your time at all — what if it actually helps you break free from your own limitations?"

In that single moment, a deeper truth dawned on me. I had always tried to make every minute more efficient, but I had never stopped to ask whether efficiency alone was truly worth chasing. Those few simple words from Miss Lily quietly reshaped the way I think — and even now, they continue to guide me through countless difficulties and hurdles whenever I feel stuck.
