## The story

I've always been curious about how people think about time and its value. This curiosity deepened after a meaningful conversation with my best friend, Jola, one morning. She posed a thought-provoking question: "Do you know why poverty and wealth exist in this world? It's not as unfair as people claim; the truth is……" That single question ignited something inside me — a burning desire to reflect more deeply on life — and prompted me to embark on a journey of self-discovery.

### Memories of a Sincere Friendship

That morning, I was gently awakened by the warm golden light of dawn streaming through my window. I suddenly remembered that I had an appointment with Jola. When I arrived at the café, the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee welcomed me. To my surprise, Jola was already there, waiting with a warm smile. I greeted her cheerfully, and as I sat down, I couldn't help but notice how much she had changed. There was a quiet confidence about her that hadn't been there before.

With a grateful heart, she said, "I'm so glad I've changed." Then she began to share her thoughts openly. "Ever since I met you, I've come to understand something important: life is not a challenge to escape from. Learning is not just about passing exams, and practice is not just about winning competitions……

I used to spend entire days studying, all for the sake of making a good impression on the adults around me. But here's what I didn't realize: once I finally started receiving the praise I had always longed for, I looked around and discovered that I had lost something far more valuable — my friendships, my sense of joy, and even the dreams I had once wished upon a star had quietly disappeared.

In my pursuit of good grades, I couldn't make time for my loved ones, or even for myself. I was unable to find inner peace, constantly overwhelmed by pressure and fear."

### A Reminder: Cherish Your Life, Time, and Memories

Life is a long and winding path of growth. Ultimately, we study for ourselves — not for our teachers, our parents, or anyone else. Although life is surrounded by danger, uncertainty, and countless hurdles that we must face and overcome, it remains rich with love, hope, and the incredible drive to thrive.

Life is short, and time is precious. So do the things that truly matter to you. Accompany the people who fill your heart with joy, and savor every moment that brings a smile to your face. Cherish your life, your time, and the memories you create along the way. You are worthy of embarking on a path that is entirely your own. And always remember — you are far more capable than you think!
